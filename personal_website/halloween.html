Halloween:

Trunk-or-Treat

Do you want to let your kids go Trick-or-Treating but are afraid of letting them wander around the city after dark? Well fear no more! This event will be held at the LDS Church on 612 Boyd-Kennedy Pkwy. And there will be cars ready to hand out candy to all your ghoulish/fairy-like little candy-hoarders. So they will be able to Trick-or-Treat as they love while you do not have to worry about scetchy neighborhoods. Even if you do not have any candy we would still love to see you there. Now have a ghoulish night!
